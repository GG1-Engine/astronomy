About This Feature

Your location is used to calculate:

  • Altitude & azimuth — where in the sky the object is right now
  • Horizon visibility — whether it's currently above or below your horizon
  • Transit altitude — how high it gets at its best
  • Rise/set azimuth — compass direction to look

Location is stored locally on this server. Geocoding uses Nominatim / OpenStreetMap. Calculations use standard astronomical algorithms (no external service required after location is set).
